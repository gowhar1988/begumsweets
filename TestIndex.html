About Us

Founded in 2020, Begum Sweets & Snacks was started by two young and energetic women entrepreneurs from North Chennai with a strong vision to build a trusted homemade food brand.

With passion, dedication, and determination, they stepped into the business world to create a name for themselves. Today, they proudly serve many happy and satisfied customers across Chennai.

We are specialized in authentic Chennai Muslim Style Chicken Biryani and Mutton Biryani, along with our signature sweets like Gulaab Jamun, Sweet Poori, and Ladoo for festivals, special events, marriages, and parties.

All our sweets, snacks, and biryanis are lovingly prepared by experienced family women, ensuring authentic homemade taste using traditional cooking methods.

We strictly use pure sunflower oil and premium quality ghee. We never use chemicals or artificial additives. Health and hygiene are our top priorities.

At Begum Sweets & Snacks, we don’t just cook food — we serve taste with trust, purity, and care.

Registration & Licenses

Begum Sweets & Snacks is a legally registered food business under the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI). We comply with all food safety regulations and hygiene standards as required by law.

Our FMCG products including sweets, snacks, biryani, and Nannari syrup are prepared, packed, and distributed following approved food safety guidelines.

We strictly adhere to quality control measures, safe handling practices, and clean manufacturing processes to ensure customer health and satisfaction.

Transparency, authenticity, and regulatory compliance remain our top priorities as we continue serving customers with trust and purity.
